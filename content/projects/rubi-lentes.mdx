## Overview

Rubi Lentes is a luxury e-commerce catalog for an Argentine boutique selling sunglasses, bags, and wallets from premium brands (Miu Miu, Prada, Chanel, Dior, Louis Vuitton). Instead of a traditional shopping cart, the platform uses **WhatsApp as the primary sales channel** — users browse the catalog and purchase directly through chat.

## Technical Highlights

- **AI Chatbot Integration**: Google Gemini (`gemini-2.0-flash-lite`) powers a customer-facing assistant that answers product questions, calculates prices with a safe math evaluator, and schedules showroom appointments.
- **Dual Database Architecture**: Firebase (Firestore, Auth, Storage) for products and Supabase for user registration and contacts, with localStorage fallback for resilience.
- **Premium UI/UX**: Framer Motion animations, parallax effects, luxury color palette (gold, ruby, dark), and brand-segmented product pages across three tiers (Premium, Plus, Standard).
- **WhatsApp Commerce Funnel**: Floating button + application form both route to WhatsApp with pre-filled messages including user data.
- **Transaction Emails**: Welcome email via Resend with branded HTML template.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Product CRUD, stock management with in/low/out status, image upload to Firebase Storage, and one-click database seeding.
